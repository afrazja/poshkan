Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 4, 2026

Poshkan is a stock monitoring and portfolio practice app. This policy explains what information the app collects, how it is used, and the choices available to users.

Information We Collect

  • Account information, such as email address and profile details provided during sign up.
  • Portfolio information entered by the user, including watchlists, paper trades, real portfolio tracker entries, alerts, and trade history.
  • Technical information needed to operate the app, such as authentication session data and basic server logs.

How We Use Information

  • To provide account access and save each user's watchlists, portfolios, alerts, settings, and history.
  • To display stock quotes, charts, related news, and portfolio performance features.
  • To improve app reliability, security, and user experience.

Third-Party Services

Poshkan uses third-party services to operate the app, including Supabase for authentication and database storage, Render for hosting, and market/news data providers for stock-related information. These services may process data according to their own privacy policies.

Paper Trading and Real Portfolio Tracker

Poshkan is for monitoring, learning, and practice. Paper trading uses virtual money only. The Real Portfolio Tracker lets users manually track their own holdings, but Poshkan does not connect to brokerage accounts or execute real-money trades.

Data Sharing

We do not sell personal information. Data is shared only with service providers needed to operate the app, or when required by law.

Data Security

We use reasonable technical measures to protect user data. No online service can guarantee complete security, so users should keep their login credentials private.

User Choices

Users can edit or remove watchlists, portfolio entries, alerts, paper API keys, and trade history inside the app. Users may also contact us to request account or data deletion.
