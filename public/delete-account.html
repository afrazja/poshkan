Delete Your Account

Last updated: June 4, 2026

Poshkan users can request deletion of their account and associated app data at any time.

Request account deletion

How To Request Deletion

  1. Send an email to afz.javan@gmail.com.
  2. Use the subject line: Poshkan Account Deletion Request.
  3. Include the email address used for your Poshkan account.

Data That Will Be Deleted

  • Your Poshkan account profile.
  • Your saved watchlists and stock groups.
  • Your paper portfolio, paper trades, alerts, and trade history.
  • Your real portfolio tracker entries, real watchlist, alerts, and related history.
  • Your generated paper API keys.

Data That May Be Retained

Some limited records may be retained temporarily if required for security, fraud prevention, legal compliance, backup recovery, or service logs. Retained records are not used to continue providing the app account after deletion is complete.

Processing Time

We aim to complete deletion requests within 30 days after verifying the account email.

Account deletion is permanent. Deleted portfolio, watchlist, alert, and trade history data cannot be restored.
